Important Notes Before Taking the MBTI Test

1. Participants must answer questions honestly and independently to obtain accurate results.

2. Personality analysis reflects your personality tendencies, not your knowledge, skills, or experience.

3. The personality type descriptions provided by MBTI are for participants to identify their personality type. Personality types are neither good nor bad, only different. Each personality type has its own strengths and advantages as well as weaknesses and areas for improvement. Understanding your strengths and weaknesses can help you leverage your talents while avoiding the disadvantages of your personality, enabling better interpersonal interactions and decision-making.

4. There are 61 questions; there are no right or wrong answers. Please choose based on your actual situation.

Test results are for reference only!
